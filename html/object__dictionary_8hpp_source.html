134/* From CiA402, - State coding
135 Statusword | PDS FSA state
136xxxx xxxx x0xx 0000 | Not ready to switch on
137xxxx xxxx x1xx 0000 | Switch on disabled
138xxxx xxxx x01x 0001 | Ready to switch on
139xxxx xxxx x01x 0011 | Switched on
140xxxx xxxx x01x 0111 | Operation enabled
141xxxx xxxx x00x 0111 | Quick stop active
142xxxx xxxx x0xx 1111 | Fault reaction active
143xxxx xxxx x0xx 1000 | Fault
144*/
